Rain Water Harvesting


Rainwater harvesting is the practice of collecting and storing rainwater for various uses. It reduces water scarcity by utilizing a natural resource, lessening the burden on conventional water sources. This sustainable technique helps mitigate floods and erosion while supporting greener landscapes. It cuts costs by lowering water bills and aids in sustainable gardening. Rainwater is free of chemicals, making it ideal for non-potable purposes. It eases strain on infrastructure and promotes self-sufficiency. By capturing rain where it falls, this practice conserves water, benefiting both individuals and the environment.
